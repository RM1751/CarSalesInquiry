==========================================
   📸 NEW DASHBOARD FEATURES GUIDE
==========================================

We've added a powerful Admin Dashboard to manage photos!
Access it by clicking "Dashboard" in the navigation menu.

─────────────────────────────────────────
1️⃣  CLIENT DELIVERY PHOTOS SLIDER
─────────────────────────────────────────

WHAT IT DOES:
- Upload photos taken with clients during car delivery
- These photos automatically run as a background slideshow
- Displayed on the hero section behind the main content

HOW TO USE:
1. Go to Dashboard section
2. Click "Add Photo" in the Client Delivery Photos card
3. Select photos from your device
4. Photos will automatically appear in the slider
5. Adjust slider speed (3, 5, 8, or 10 seconds)
6. Toggle on/off as needed

TIPS:
- Take happy photos with clients holding their new car keys
- Group photos with family members work great
- In front of the new car is the perfect backdrop
- Photos auto-save to your browser

─────────────────────────────────────────
2️⃣  CAR CATEGORY PHOTOS
─────────────────────────────────────────

WHAT IT DOES:
- Upload actual car photos for each model
- Photos replace the generic icons on car cards
- Customers see real images of each car

AVAILABLE CATEGORIES:
- Alto, S-Presso, WagonR
- Celerio, Swift, Dzire
- BREZZA, Ertiga, Eeco

HOW TO USE:
1. Go to Dashboard section
2. Select car model from the category tabs
3. Click "Upload" or drag & drop photos
4. Photos appear on the car cards automatically
5. Upload multiple angles of each car

TIPS:
- Use showroom photos for best quality
- Front 3/4 angle works best for car cards
- Interior shots can also be uploaded
- Clear, well-lit photos attract more customers

─────────────────────────────────────────
3️⃣  SAVING YOUR CHANGES
─────────────────────────────────────────

IMPORTANT:
- All photos are saved to browser's local storage
- Click "Save Changes" button to persist data
- Data stays saved even after closing browser
- Clear browser cache will reset all photos

HOW TO BACKUP:
- Currently, photos are stored locally in your browser
- For permanent backup, keep original photo files safe
- Future version may include cloud storage option
